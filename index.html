About Calliopean Club

THE HOME FOR DEBATING

Founded with the purpose of providing a home for fair, charitable debate on Discord, Calliopean Club is an intellectual discussion forum that encourages discussions on a variety of topics pertaining to politics, philosophy, economics, and science. No view is unwelcome, and no claim left unchallenged.
